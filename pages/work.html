Clashot

Clashot is a photobank in your pocket, that helps you to earn money by taking photos and selling them online. No need to agonize over looking for subjects and objects for your photos: in this app, you can always look at what your friends and other Clashot users are shooting, as well as look through photos deemed the best and most popular.
